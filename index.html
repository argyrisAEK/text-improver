Text Improver

Upload a text file or directly type/paste your text

How to Use:

  1. Upload a .txt file
  2. Click the "Highlight Phrases Needing Improvement" button
  3. Phrases that could be improved will be highlighted in yellow
  4. Click on any highlighted phrase to see specific suggestions
  5. Edit the text directly in the box or apply suggested changes
  6. Or click "Get Improved Text" to automatically improve the entire text
  7. Use the "Show Before & After Comparison" button to see what was changed
